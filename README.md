# TIRO-2022
TIRO - Time Is Running Out : Productivity app and widget for Android

The purpose of this app is to let you know how much remaining time you have in your day.

Just select the time you go to sleep and how much hours is reserved in your day (ex: eating).
Your remaining time display is ready, just click on the button to add a widget on your home screen.

**Now you're motivated to do productive things!**

The widget uses the alarm manager to update. It updates every 3min to preserve the battery.

If the phone is on battery saver mode, the alarm manager won't work and the widget won't update. You'll have to disable battery optimisation in the settings of your device.
